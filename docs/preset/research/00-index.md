# Beyond Honeycomb — Company Introduction Research Package

> A fact-checked research base for building a general company-introduction deck about **Beyond Honeycomb** (비욘드허니컴), the Seoul-based food-tech startup behind the **GRILL X** AI grilling robot. All facts below were gathered from public web sources, cross-checked across multiple independent sources, and the most important claims were adversarially verified.

This folder (`docs/preset/research/`) holds seven topic briefs plus this index. Companion images live in `../images/research_images/` (see [Image Library](#image-library)).

## Verification Summary

The ten most decision-critical claims were each checked by two independent adversarial fact-checkers (a claim is only "confirmed" when neither refutes it).

- **9 of 10 confirmed** — founding year/HQ, CEO identity & Samsung pedigree, both funding rounds, cumulative funding, NAVER D2SF + cafeteria deployment, the molecular-sensor technology, the Hanam Pig MOU, and the CES 2022 demo.
- **1 refuted (a bundled claim)** — "500,000+ dataset **and** 144 servings/hour **and** 10-min install." The dataset (500k+) and the ~10-min install are supported; **the "144 servings/hour" figure is not** — see below.

### Contested figures — use these numbers

| Figure | Use this | Avoid / explain |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| **Throughput** | **"up to ~80 grilled servings per hour"** (2025 NRA Show + MLex Dec 2025; CB Insights customer page says ~80–100/hr) | The "**144/hour**" figure traces to a single AI-generated aggregator (Extruct) that also misstates funding. Some reseller listings say "up to 120/hr." Safe phrasing: *"up to ~80/hour"*. |
| **Cumulative funding** | **~KRW 19.6B (~US$14.1M)** — company/Korean-press figure | Aggregators show less (CB Insights ~US$12.78M; Tracxn ~US$12.6M) because they use historical FX rates, can't price the undisclosed seed, and differ on round count. Not a contradiction — a methodology gap. |
| **Install time** | **~10 minutes, no construction** (widely cited) | The current grillx.ai FAQ now answers "less than 30 minutes." Either is defensible; "about 10 minutes" is the marketing standard. |
| **CEO name** | **Jeong Hyun-ki** / "Steve Jung" (use both once, then "Steve Jung") | Also romanized "Hyungki Jung" / "Jeong Hyeon-gi" — all the same person. |
| **2023 Series A investors** | LB Investment (lead), KDB, Devsisters Ventures, **POSCO Technology Investment, BA Partners** | English summaries sometimes drop BA Partners and rename POSCO as "POSCO Venture Capital." |

## Methodology & Provenance

- **Collected:** 2026-06-18.
- **Tools:** exa (semantic discovery) + firecrawl (clean page/image extraction) + web search/fetch.
- **Process:** 7 parallel research agents (one per angle) + 2 image-scout agents, followed by a 20-agent adversarial verification pass (2 independent checkers × 10 critical claims).
- **Source discipline:** primary sources (company sites, dated press releases, named-author news such as Chosun, Wowtale, VentureSquare, MLex, Platform, AVING) were weighted above automated profile aggregators (CB Insights, Tracxn, Extruct), which were used only for corroboration and explicitly flagged where they conflict.
- Each topic brief carries its own inline citations and a dated Sources list. Treat any fact marked *low confidence* in a brief as needing a primary-source check before external use.
