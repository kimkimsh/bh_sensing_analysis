BH · SENSING ANALYSIS04
The Approach

Score it two ways — then measure the agreement.

With no labels, "more accurate" is unprovable. So we hold the doneness logic constant and vary only how the meat region is found — then ask: where do the two methods see the same meat?

METHOD A

ONNX ROI

meatSegNet AI segmentation finds the meat region from 5 LED channels.

+
SHARED

One doneness cascade

The exact live charbroiler rule — identical for both. The only variable is the ROI.

+
METHOD B

Rule ROI

Spectral rule-based morphology finds the meat region from raw bands.

Agreement, not accuracy.  We never claim a winner. We show where the two methods align and where they diverge — an honest signal engineers can act on.

What We Found

Two very different methods, strong agreement.

3.0 pts

The takeaway

The AI ROI tracks the rule-based ROI closely while recovering a slightly cleaner boundary — without any menu-specific retuning. Where they diverge is now visible, measurable, and reviewable instead of a matter of opinion.
